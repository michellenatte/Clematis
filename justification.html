    Paper Prototype Testing Scenarios

    Identified Problems:

    Saying socials in the down right hand corner was confusing, as our tester thought it would be a social platform for our users.

    Experience Reviews were not found easily on the about us page.

    Positive feedback:

    Homepage was visually pleasing and eye-catching. It invited the tester to explore our website more.

    The Website is very easy to understand and userfriendly.

    The amount of information given about our brand is perfect, like for example descriptions of our products, our vision and experience reviews.

    Website improvements:

    based on user testing, we adjusted our footer to make it easier for the user to found us on social media.

    Moreover, we decided to keep our page as visual as possible. We made use of a lot of pictures and tried to keep written text down to a minimum in order to engage the visitors.

    Another change we made was adding our team to the pages instead of review articles. We made this choice to bring ourselves closer to our target group in order to be more personal and trustworthy.
