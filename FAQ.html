Frequently  Asked Questions (FAQ):

Clematis is a Brand that focuses on helping people with stress and troubles of their daily life. We want to give our users a possibility to sit down and relax by drinking our tea and coffee products.

Four managers who used to always get burn-outs were tired of being stressed all the time and seeing their colleagues and friends suffer under work pressure, life pressure, social obligations, etc. We wanted to remind the people that there is nothing more important in your life then yourself. So in order to make people take their well deserved breaks, we decided to create this brand and it's relaxing products.

"Clematis" is "a climbing plant of the buttercup family which bears white, pink, or purple flowers and feathery seeds. Several kinds are cultivated as ornamentals."

Clematis is more then just a tea or coffee brand. We really care about people's mental healths and are constantly working on expanding our business by adding workshops, mindfullness videos, a forum, etc. There is so much more coming!

Our Tea and Coffee is fair-trade from the beautiful countries Brasil, Honduras, Columbia and Indonesia.
